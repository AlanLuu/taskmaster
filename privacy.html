PRIVACY POLICY

(Last revised March 2023)

Information collected for all visitors:

  • Log files containing your IP address may be collected.

How this information is used:

  • Log files are only used for analytics purposes and are only saved for a short period of time before being deleted.
  • None of this information is used for tracking, marketing, or commercial purposes whatsoever.

Information collected for users with an account

  • When you sign up for an account, you are prompted to provide the following information:
    • Username
    • Password
    • Email address (optional)
  • Tasks and any other content you upload using your account (hereinafter referred to as "User-Generated Content")

How this information is used:

  • This information is used only for the purposes of providing you with the functionality of this website.
  • If you decide to provide an email address, it is used for the sole purpose of allowing you to reset your account's password and is NOT used for any other purpose whatsoever.
  • Any User-Generated Content uploaded to this website will not be analyzed except for administrative purposes or when required by law.
  • Great effort is taken to secure any information that is collected.
  • None of this information is used for tracking, marketing, or commercial purposes whatsoever.

Usage of Google APIs

This website uses several Google APIs such as Google Fonts and reCAPTCHA. Review Google's privacy policy .

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy is subject to change at any time without notice, and your continued usage of this website is deemed an acceptance of those changes. The latest version of this privacy policy will always be reflected on this page.
